# 01. Find and fix a common-sense flaw

## Context

A small order service computes an order total. It has one design flaw. Run the common-sense
check to find the flaw, then fix it.

Lesson: The common-sense check.

## What to do

1. Open `src/` and read the order service.
2. Find the flaw and fix it.
3. Fill in `SOLUTION.md`.

## Rules

- The reviewer reads your code, it does not run it. Keep the total-computing function readable
  and callable the same way.
- Do not weaken the task by deleting the catalog or hard-coding a total.

You are graded on your explanation of the flaw as much as on the fix.
